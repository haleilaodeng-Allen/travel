Best Season

  • 浮潜看曼塔鱼和鲸鲨Snorkel with manta rays & whale sharks
  • 海底餐厅享用浪漫晚餐Underwater restaurant dining
  • 日落巡游，看天空变色Sunset cruise · Magical sky colors

出行信息
Getting There
  • 飞行约3-4小时（吉隆坡/新加坡）~3-4hrs from KL / Singapore
  • 抵达马累后转快艇或水上飞机Speedboat or seaplane from Malé
  • 免签入境，落地签30天Visa on arrival · 30 days free

出行贴士
Tips
  • 货币为拉菲亚，美元通用USD widely accepted
  • 防晒必备，赤道阳光强烈Strong equatorial sun · Sunscreen essential
  • 岛上禁酒，度假村内可饮Alcohol only within resort premises
  • 带足现金，小岛ATM少Bring cash · Limited ATMs on islands
